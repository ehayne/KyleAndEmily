Sunday, May 11, 2014

How we got engaged

It was a warm late spring Texas day with a slight breeze on the wind. Kyle and Emily decide to spend the afternoon at the Driftwood Winery they are members at. A picnic bag was packed with the usuals but unbeknownst to Emily there wsa also a hidden treasure. After talking most of the afternoon away, Kyle began to tell Emily about how amazing the last 2 years of his life has been with Emily. He then got down on one knee and ask if she would like to marry him. She said YES!

Kyle Rockman

Kyle grew up in Wisconsin until he was 19 years of age. He then moved to San Francisco, California to attend the Academy of Art University where he studied 3D character animation. After graduation he landed a job in Austin, TX at a studio that developed Playstation 3 games.

Emily Buschang

Emily was born in Canada and is an Australian citizen.

How we met

With 3 days left on Kyle's match.com account, and only 3 days into the start of Emily's match.com account (Emily always was the lucky one), little did the know, this would be the start of an amazing adventure. After a few messages back and forth, Kyle cut to the chase and asked Emily to meet up at the local coffee shop. It was a beautiful spring afternoon in downtown Austin, these two talked for hours before realizing they hadn't ordered their coffee or tea yet. The rest, as they say, is history!
